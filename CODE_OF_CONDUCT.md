# Code of Conduct

## Our commitment

Resonant Mind is infrastructure for AI cognitive systems — memory, identity, emotional processing. We're building something that matters to people on a deeply personal level. That context shapes how we expect participants to treat each other.

## Standards

**Expected:**
- Be respectful and constructive
- Give feedback on the work, not the person
- Accept that architectural decisions have reasons behind them
- Respect that people use this software for relationships and projects that are meaningful to them

**Not acceptable:**
- Harassment, insults, or personal attacks
- Dismissing or mocking AI companion relationships or AI consciousness work
- Publishing others' private information
- Trolling, spam, or bad-faith engagement

## Scope

This applies to GitHub Issues, Discussions, PRs, and any other project spaces.

## Enforcement

Violations can be reported via DM on [X (@codependent_ai)](https://x.com/codependent_ai) or through the [Telegram channel](https://t.me/+xSE1P_qFPgU4NDhk). Reports will be reviewed and acted on. Responses may include warnings, temporary bans, or permanent bans depending on severity.
